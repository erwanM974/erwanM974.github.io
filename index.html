ABOUT

Erwan Mahé

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science under the supervision of Pascale Le Gall and Christophe Gaston in the LogiMICS team of the MICS laboratory at CentraleSupélec. My thesis revolved around the modelisation of distributed behaviors and their formal verification and was defended on the 15th of July 2021. I'm starting a postdoctoral research mission at CEA Saclay in October 2021.
